Helen is a current Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Patrick P.C. Lee in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. She is also a member of the ADSLab. She received her B.Eng. in Computer Engineering from the same department in 2013. Her research interests include reliability and efficiency of flash-based storage system.

Research

Projects

  • LSM-tree-based key-value store: (Coming soon)
  • MemEC: Erasure-code in-memory key-value store which optimizes in performance for small key-value objects. She mainly involves in implementing the erasure coding component and performance evaluations of the prototype.
  • EPLog: Elastic Parity Logging scheme for improving endurance and performance of SSD arrays. She mainly involves in implementing and evaluating the prototype
  • CodFS: Parity logging with reserved space for mitigating update overheads in erasue-coded file system. She mainly involves in evaluation and field deployment of the file system in third-party clusters.
