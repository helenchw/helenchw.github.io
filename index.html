Helen is now an R&D Engineer at Unissoft Tech, working on the projects related to cloud-based storage systems. Her main interest lies in building reliable and efficient storage solutions.

She completed her PhD study in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Patrick P.C. Lee in the ADSLab of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong. She received her B.Eng. in Computer Engineering from the same department in 2013.
